My spam corpus has just crossed 60,000 messages, so it's time for a quick retrospective on the last 10,000 messages. For that group, bogofilter looks to be around 87% at catching spam with a 99% "bogosity" threshold with no false positives, and relaxing the "bogosity" threshold to 90% would have made it 99% effective.
