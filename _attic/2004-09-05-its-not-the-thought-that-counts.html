Someone was recently described to me as an "idea guy", and on reflection, I suppose that it's true -- the person in question does have a number of ideas. Unfortunately, he is unable to discern a good idea from a bad idea, which makes him completely useless. If someone is a "good idea guy", then I'm interested.
