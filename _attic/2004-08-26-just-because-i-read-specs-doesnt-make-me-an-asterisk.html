I believe that specifications should be unambiguous for a large, useful subset of the universe of possible instances and then completely silent otherwise. (People should know better than to try for full coverage...) It is incumbent on the initial architects of a specification to ensure that the useful subset is well-chosen and then up to the subsequent clients of the specification to ensure that dependencies are restricted to the unambiguous subset.
