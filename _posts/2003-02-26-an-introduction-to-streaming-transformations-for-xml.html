Our STX article is up!

The article that Oliver Becker, Petr Cimprich and I wrote about STX is up on XML.com:

An Introduction to Streaming Transformations for XML.An introduction to Streaming Transformations for XML (STX), a template-based XML transformation language that operates on streams of SAX events. STX bears a strong resemblance to XSLT 1.0, the tree-driven transformation language for XML, but offers unique features and advantages for some applications.
