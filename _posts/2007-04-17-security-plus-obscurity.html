So, is that last character a one, a lowercase ell, a capital ell, or a pipe? (And, for that matter, what about people who are visually impaired?) Maybe a little more thought about the font is warranted. After getting a "our system is down, try again later" error while trying the possibilities, I tried again later only to be told that I'd exhausted my guesses. I'll point out that it would be simpler (and $0.30 cheaper) to assign a password to or collect a password from people as they open their accounts.

Time for a new bank.
