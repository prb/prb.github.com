It's time for predictions, resolutions, and retrospectives. My one-sentence plan for 2004 is Do less and accomplish more.

From The Effective Executive (which is older than I am) by Peter Drucker:

If there is any one “secret” of effectiveness, it is concentration. Effective executives do first things first and they do one thing at a time. [...] [T]o get even that half-day or those two weeks of really productive time requires self-discipline and an iron determination to say “No.”
